# Multi-functional Smart Street Lamp Idea

## Concept Overview
A street lamp designed with advanced highway safety, green energy harvesting, and emergency functionalities:

- **Savonius Wind Turbine** at the center of the pole, one end connected to a dynamo (harvesting energy from fast-moving highway vehicles), other end powering an **Air Purifier** placed above.
- **Solar Panel** at the top for continuous energy generation.
- **Energy Storage**: Wind and solar energy stored in batteries.
- **LED Street Lamps** powered by harvested energy.
- **Two 360Â° WiFi/5G Cameras** at the pole top for live footage, accident detection, and alerts:
    - Sends instant alerts to police stations, hospitals, ambulances.
    - Notifies drones for fast oxygen/medicine delivery at accident sites.
    - Includes AI for live detection of accidents and driver drowsiness or unconsciousness, especially at night.
- **Emergency Calling Booth** at the bottom, powered by solar and wind.
- **Speed Cameras** using smart AI/IoT vision systems:
    - Detects speeding vehicles.
    - Sends alerts to drivers, especially at night or when drowsy.
- **Placement**: At the center of highways for optimal wind turbine rotation, air purifying efficacy, and coverage.

## Features & Potential Impact
- Green energy harvesting and sustainability.
- Real-time accident detection and life-saving rapid response.
- Enhanced driver awareness and safety (smart alerts).
- Integrated air purification for healthier traffic environments.
- Emergency communication access for quick help.
